# MVP v16 Text Polish

This pass removes or replaces several lines that sounded emotional but did not earn emotion through action.

## Updated objective

- 夜粥街 objective: 饮一碗热粥

## Text direction

The revised text avoids slogan-like emotional statements and prefers:

- concrete objects
- visible action
- simple task wording
- character behavior
- physical consequence
- silence and pacing

The goal is to let emotion come from play, images, pauses, character behavior, sound and consequence, instead of telling the player what to feel.
